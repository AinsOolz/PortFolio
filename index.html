/01/
WEB DESIGN
Travelly - Hotel Booking

This project was undertaken as part of one of my modules at Republic Polytechnic, where my team and I were tasked with developing a website of our choice. The requirements included implementing user registration and login features, along with role-based functionalities for both administrators and standard users. For our project, we chose to create Travelly, a hotel-booking platform designed to offer a seamless and intuitive experience.

 

In such collaborative work, understanding one’s role and coordinating effectively with team members becomes essential. Each individual’s contribution must integrate harmoniously with the others, ensuring that separate functions operate as a cohesive whole. It is important to support one another throughout the process, while also remaining responsible for one’s own assigned tasks.

DESIGN FUNDAMENTAL
Study on the Fundamentals of Design

This collection showcases the work I produced while studying the fundamentals of design at Republic Polytechnic. It includes logo explorations, typographic studies, moodboards, and experiments with AI-assisted design tools available in software such as Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop—demonstrating both the creation of AI-generated elements and the techniques used to refine and adapt them.

 

The experience proved truly illuminating, offering a broad introduction to the many mediums and disciplines a designer must be well-versed in—ranging from typography to emerging AI technologies. Throughout my studies, I found great enthusiasm in discovering new forms of creative expression, and I remain eager to continue expanding my skills, embracing future tools and methods with an open and curious mind.

/03/
IMMERSIVE TECHNOLOGY
Diving into VR - Baker’s Bizarre Bake-Off

This project is a VR experience I developed for my final assessment in Immersive Technology. The task required the creation of an “escape room”-style environment featuring four distinct puzzles, all built around a theme of my choosing. I elected to design a whimsical, fantasy-inspired baking adventure, complete with a rotating puzzle, several logic challenges, and a finale in which the player concludes the experience by baking a magical cake.

 

It proved to be an unexpectedly adventurous foray into virtual reality. Throughout the module, I had to adapt to a development process far removed from my usual comfort zone, building a functional game from the ground up with limited time and resources. Though the journey was demanding, it ultimately strengthened my ability to adjust swiftly and effectively when confronted with unfamiliar challenges.

MOBILE APP DEVELOPMENT
Steps into Mobile Development

This collection presents my work in mobile application development, completed as part of one of my modules at Republic Polytechnic. Over the course of the module, I explored a diverse range of app concepts, including an income and expense tracker, a 3D model catalogue, and even a Pokémon-themed quiz—each offering its own set of creative and technical challenges.

 

Drawing upon the adaptability I developed during my earlier venture into VR, I approached mobile development with greater confidence and readiness to learn. This module broadened my familiarity with various programming languages and provided valuable insight into the wider landscape of app creation, further enriching my journey as a growing developer.
